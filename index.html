Who I Am

About Me

I’m a data scientist based in Bucharest, Romania, with a deep academic foundation — a Master of Science in Physics and Mathematics — and a genuine passion for applying mathematical rigour to real-world AI challenges.

My work spans financial forecasting, NLP-based chatbots, computer vision pipelines, and medical predictions. I care about the full lifecycle — from EDA and model development to clean deployment using Flask, Streamlit, and Docker.

I currently also teach IGCSE Mathematics at ISB Bucharest and run an “Step to AI Engineering” club, introducing students to foundational AI concepts through hands-on projects.

I’m actively expanding into LLMs and Generative AI — having built RAG chatbots using LangChain, FAISS, and OpenAI — and I’m always eager for new challenges at the intersection of data, intelligence, and impact.
